[h2]Configuration options[/h2] [list] [*][b]Replace Weapons[/b] When enabled, replace original enemy weapons with new ones. Allows to use C-UAE along site Random Enemy Weapons mod which provides different way of distributing weapons in Grand Chien. [*][b]Add Weapon Components[/b] When enabled, weapons are strengthened with attachments. Better attachments are given to higher-level enemies. This also causes the dropped weapons to have attachments. [*][b]Replace Armor[/b] When enabled, replace the original enemy armor with new ones. [*][b]Affect Militia[/b] When enabled, mod affects Militia squads. [*][b]Extra Grenades Chance[/b] C-UAE allows enemies to get extra grenades. This option modifies the chance an enemy will get extra granades. [*][b]Armament Progression Speed Factor[/b] Default: 0. Alters how fast better armaments and better weapons attachments will start appearing on enemies. It is not a difficulty option. Higher values are meant for players who want to gear up faster. [/list]
